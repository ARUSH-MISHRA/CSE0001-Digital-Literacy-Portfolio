## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this task was to understand how cybercrimes affect people in everyday digital life, especially students, and to learn practical ways to stay safe online.

## 🛡️ Topic Chosen
For this task, I selected:

### **UPI / Online Payment Fraud**

Through this task, I learned that cybercrime often succeeds not only because of technical loopholes, but also because of a lack of awareness and hurried decisions. This activity helped me better understand digital safety and the importance of being alert while using online platforms and payment systems.
